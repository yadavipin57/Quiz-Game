Information

  • Press "Start Quiz" to begin.
  • The quiz consist of questions followed by four alternate answers, one of which is correct.
  • You have to select the one which is correct.
  • For every correct answer you will be rewarded 1 point and for incorrect or unattempted questions you will be rewarded 0 point.
  • You can press "Next" button to go to next question.
  • Keep in mind, when "Next" button is clicked you cannot go to previous question.
  • You can click "Submit" button to submit the quiz. The "Submit" button is always enabled.
  • You can click "Quit" button to quit the game. The "Quit" button is always enabled
